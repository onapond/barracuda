# Barracuda Copy Team Cycle 1
Version: 2026-03-17

Purpose
This is the first Copy Team deliverable for Barracuda.
It rewrites the highest-priority routes into shorter, clearer, Korean-first brand copy.

Scope
- `/`
- `/coffee`
- `/store`
- `/visit`

Copy direction
- calm
- precise
- spatial
- brand-first

What this copy is trying to fix
- too many long paragraphs
- repeated brand claims
- weak differentiation caused by abstract language
- unstable line breaks caused by oversized sentence length

Shared rules
- short first
- one message per section
- no decorative filler
- no repeated "not just a cafe" framing
- no oversized emotional prose

## CTA Cleanup

Use these labels consistently.

- 방문 정보 보기
- 커피 보기
- 공간 보기
- 메뉴 보기
- 길 찾기
- 인스타그램
- 원두 보기
- 카테고리 보기
- 브랜드 보기

Avoid these patterns
- 자세히 보기
- 더 알아보기
- 경험해 보기
- 지금 확인하기

Reason
- generic CTA wording weakens brand specificity and does not help scanning.

---

## Prohibited Phrases

Do not reuse these patterns in the next draft.

- 단순 카페가 아닙니다
- 커피와 공간, 그리고 경험
- 조용하고 정제된 분위기 속에서
- 자연스럽게 이어집니다
- 브랜드의 무드를 보여줍니다
- 감도를 담았습니다
- 하나의 결로 연결됩니다

Why these are blocked
- They are either overused, too abstract, or too similar across sections.

---

## Design Team Handoff

This copy set assumes the following typography behavior.

- Hero title should hold at 1 to 2 lines on mobile.
- Hero body should stay within 1 to 2 short sentences.
- Section titles should mostly fit within 2 lines on mobile.
- Section descriptions should usually be 1 short paragraph.
- Card descriptions should remain single-line or near single-line.

High-risk lines for responsive treatment
- 커피와 공간을 한 결로 엮습니다.
- 머무는 시간이 인상을 만듭니다.
- 로스터리의 기반, 배러쿠다의 결
- 배러쿠다의 제품 세계를 미리 봅니다.

Orchestrator recommendation
- Prefer the shorter alternate hero lines if visual density remains high at `360px`.
